1. PROPERTY DESCRIPTION
The Lessor hereby leases to the Lessee the residential property located at 789 Maple Avenue, Springfield, IL 62703 (the "Premises").

2. TERM
The term of this lease shall commence on August 15, 2024, and shall continue for a period of 12 months, ending on August 14, 2025.

3. RENT
The total rent for the term of this lease shall be $1,200.00 per month, due on the 1st day of each month. The first month’s rent shall be due upon signing this Agreement.

4. SECURITY DEPOSIT
The Lessee agrees to pay a security deposit of $1,200.00 upon signing this Agreement. This deposit will be held by the Lessor and returned to the Lessee at the end of the lease term, subject to deductions for damages beyond normal wear and tear.

5. UTILITIES
The Lessee shall be responsible for all utilities associated with the Premises, including electricity, gas, water, and internet.

6. MAINTENANCE AND REPAIRS
The Lessee shall maintain the Premises in good condition and promptly notify the Lessor of any repairs needed. The Lessor shall be responsible for major repairs unless damage is due to the Lessee’s negligence.

7. TERMINATION
At the end of the lease term, the Lessee shall vacate the Premises and return all keys to the Lessor. The Lessee may terminate this Agreement with 30 days written notice to the Lessor.

8. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Illinois.
