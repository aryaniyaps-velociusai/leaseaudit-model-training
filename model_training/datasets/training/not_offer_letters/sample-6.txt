1. RENEWAL OF LEASE
This Agreement serves to renew the lease for the residential property located at 789 Maple Avenue, Springfield, IL 62703 (the "Premises") originally dated August 15, 2023.

2. TERM
The term of the renewed lease shall commence on August 15, 2024, and shall continue for a period of 12 months, ending on August 14, 2025.

3. RENT
The rent for the term of this renewed lease shall be $1,250.00 per month, due on the 1st day of each month. The first month’s rent for this renewed lease shall be due upon the start date.

4. SECURITY DEPOSIT
The original security deposit of $1,200.00 shall remain in effect for the renewed lease term.

5. UTILITIES
The Lessee shall continue to be responsible for all utilities associated with the Premises, including electricity, gas, water, and internet.

6. MAINTENANCE AND REPAIRS
The Lessee shall maintain the Premises in good condition and promptly notify the Lessor of any repairs needed. The Lessor shall be responsible for major repairs unless damage is due to the Lessee’s negligence.

7. TERMINATION
At the end of the renewed lease term, the Lessee shall vacate the Premises and return all keys to the Lessor. The Lessee may terminate this Agreement with 30 days written notice to the Lessor.

8. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Illinois.
